What is Material Design?

Material Design is a design concept and specification created by Google for the Android OS. You can see the design guide here: https://material.google.com. According to their site, the goals are to provide a design that is native to all screen sizes and to bridge the gap between virtual design and reality (to make movement and animation more realistic.)
